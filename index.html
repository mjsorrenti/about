About Me

I am a user experience designer with over 10 years of expertise designing software and interfaces for cutting edge technologies. My career has spanned design, communication, and management responsibilities with a constant focus on delivering customers and end users the best possible product. For many years I have been designing for Dassault Systèmes on complex engineering and project management applications. More recently I have taken on side projects designing and implementing business or community-oriented web sites. In all of my work, I strive to make complex things understandable and simple things fun.

I currently live in Pawtucket, Rhode Island with my wife and family. Outside of UX design, I enjoy reading up on history, word puzzles, cooking, and a nip of bourbon every once and a while.

About This Site

I designed and coded this site from scratch. Everything is implemented using HTML and CSS, primarily to see how far I could push things using pure styling code. Check out my GitHub repository for examples of JavaScript work.

Beyond the obvious goal of publicizing myself, I use this site to test out design ideas and simply to have fun. I'm always tinkering with it, so check back periodically to see what's changed. And of course feel free to let me know what you think!
