About Me

I am a user experience designer with over 10 years of expertise designing software and interfaces for cutting edge technologies. My career has spanned design, communication, and management responsibilities with a constant focus on delivering customers and end users the best possible product. For many years I have been designing for Dassault Systèmes on complex engineering and project management applications. More recently I have taken on side projects designing and implementing business or community-oriented web sites. In all of my work, I strive to make complex things understandable and simple things fun.

I currently live in Pawtucket, Rhode Island with my wife and family. Outside of UX design, I enjoy reading up on history, word puzzles, cooking, and a sip of bourbon every now and again.

About This Site

I designed and coded this site from scratch. Everything is implemented using rigorously semantic HTML (my old days as a technical writer stick with me) and CSS. There are a few lines of JavaScript to handle click events and set classes, but otherwise I'm trying to see how far I can push things using pure styling code. I have a few other projects with a heavier focus on JavaScript in my GitHub repository.

Beyond the obvious goal of publicizing myself, I use this site to test out design ideas and simply to play around. I'm always tinkering with it, so check back periodically to see what's changed. And of course feel free to let me know what you think!
